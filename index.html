01 · Business Case
The problem is real.
The market is every vehicle on India's roads.
Weeks 1–2 · Internal sign-off
Problem Statement
🚨
Emergency — Zero Contact
When a vehicle is in an accident, bystanders have no way to reach the owner's family. There is no mechanism to contact emergency services, nearby hospitals, or ambulances using vehicle information alone. Critical minutes are lost between "accident detected" and "help dispatched." Blood group, insurance, and emergency contacts are completely inaccessible. RSA providers cannot be reached instantly. The gap is life-threatening and entirely unsolved for 330M vehicles on Indian roads.
🚗
Wrong Parking — No Recourse
A vehicle blocking a driveway or gate — and no way to reach the driver. Today, the only options are: leave a paper note, wait indefinitely, or escalate to authorities. Some residents resort to leaving visiting cards on dashboards — an unsafe, uncommon practice. Approximately 10 lakh people are already using QR tags from existing companies (Sampark.me, Park+) — proving clear demand. But no solution comes with the scale, brand trust, and ecosystem that Cars24 has.
Solution — One Sticker, Two Experiences
STRANGER SCANS (Emergency / Wrong Parking)
No app needed — native phone camera scans the QR. Stranger immediately sees: vehicle registration, colour, model. Options: masked call to owner (number never shared), masked call to emergency contact provided by owner, send WhatsApp/SMS message, call RSA for towing/tyre/battery, call nearby emergency services (hospital, ambulance). All calls completely masked — zero privacy risk for both parties.
Cross-sell opportunities for stranger:
→ "Interested in buying this car?" — owner notified when enough enquiries hit; meanwhile browse similar Cars24 listings nearby. → "Get your own Smart Tag — ₹499 for 2 stickers, delivered in 2 days."
OWNER SCANS (Orbit Dashboard)
Owner scan is authenticated via C24 app login → routes to full Orbit dashboard. Smart QR Tag management lives as a dedicated section within Orbit: activate tag, add emergency contacts, manage linked stickers, view scan history (who scanned, where, when), toggle masked calling on/off.
Up-sell opportunities for owner:
→ Insurance renewal. → RSA subscription upsell. → ₹999 plan with 1-year RSA + enhanced features. → Live AI valuation — "Your car is worth ₹2.1L today." → Sell offer triggered when scan frequency is high.
Promotes Cars24's Crashfree Initiative — a sticker on every car is the cheapest way to put the C24 brand on every vehicle on India's roads.
Front sticker + rear sticker on every vehicle. Weatherproof vinyl. No app to download for strangers. Masked calling via Exotel. The physical tag is permanent brand real estate on 330M+ vehicles.

Distribution & GTM
Phase 1 - First 3 months
Bundle with GS and PDI
Launch through GS deliveries and PDI where Cars24 already controls the customer moment. This keeps CAC effectively at zero and helps validate activation, scan rates, and owner-side cross-sell.
Scale target: up to 5,000 kits/month
Phase 2 - Next 3 months
NBFC cross-sell plus direct purchase
Expand to NBFC and other physical-touchpoint channels, while also launching D2C and online purchase flows for existing Cars24 customers. Cross-sell via VI, CI, Orbit, and all high-intent touchpoints in the app.
Scale target: up to 20,000 kits/month
Phase 3 - Next 6 months
Marketplaces and quick commerce
Once the proposition is validated, open up scale channels: Amazon, Flipkart, Blinkit, Zepto, and Instamart. This is where category demand broadens, while Cars24 still retains brand and ecosystem advantages.
Scale target: up to 50,000 kits/month
0 - 3 months
Owned-channel rollout
GS, PDI, and Cars24-controlled journeys only. Best economics, fastest learning, lowest execution complexity.
3 - 6 months
Cross-sell-led expansion
NBFC touchpoints, app moments, and D2C purchase flows start broadening distribution without breaking the owned-channel advantage.
6 - 12 months
External scale channels
Marketplaces and quick-commerce become acceleration levers once activation and unit economics are already proven.

SWOT Analysis — Cars24 perspective
Strengths
  • Largest owned distribution in used cars — GS stores, PDI, app, VI/CI all at zero incremental CAC
  • Strongest brand trust in Indian used car space — no competitor has this
  • Orbit app already live with active user base — Smart Tag integrates natively
  • Biggest ecosystem for cross-sell: insurance, RSA, financing, valuation, resale — no QR tag player has any of this
  • Unmatched transaction data for AI features — real sale prices, inspection data, regional demand
  • Crashfree initiative alignment — tag on every C24 car is both safety mission and brand marketing
Opportunities
  • High margin, low effort, asset-light business — 80% gross margin with 35% EBITDA on external channels
  • TAM is every vehicle on the road — 330M+ immediately addressable
  • Ecosystem benefits: every scan is a cross-sell and upsell trigger for insurance, RSA, valuation, resale
  • Cheapest way to get physical real estate on every car on Indian roads — permanent brand impression
  • AI use cases: sell-signal trigger, insurance lapse detection, challan alert, escalation engine, parking heatmap — none of which any competitor can replicate
  • Promotes the Crashfree initiative — social impact + brand positioning in one product
Weaknesses
  • Not core to Cars24's business — risk of low internal priority and resource allocation
Threats
  • No entry barrier — low investment and high margin means the category can saturate quickly once PMF is proven. Competitors will follow fast.
  • In case of very high adoption, the government could integrate this functionality directly within FASTag infrastructure — which would break down the entire QR tag business model overnight.
